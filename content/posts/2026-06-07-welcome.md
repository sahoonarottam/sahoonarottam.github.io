For a long time I have been told to write down what I see, hear and think — as
a scientist, as an educator, and as someone who has had the good fortune to
work at the intersection of laboratories, classrooms and policy rooms.

This little corner of the web is where I will try to do that.

## What you will find here

I do not intend to write in a hurry. When I do write, the topics will probably
fall into a few familiar buckets:

- **The joy of science** — the small wonders that started me off, and the ones
  I still meet, half a century in.
- **Notes from outreach** — what works, what does not, and what we learn
  every time we put a telescope or a 3D printer in front of a curious child.
- **Policy &amp; ecosystems** — reflections from years of advisory work on how
  India can build research, innovation and entrepreneurship at the scale our
  young population deserves.
- **People** — short tributes to colleagues, students and mentors who have
  shaped me.

## A request

If something here makes you pause, write back. I am most interested in
conversations — not pronouncements. The address book is on the
[contact section](index.html#contact) of the home page.

Until the next one,

*Narottam*
